                <p><strong>OVERVIEW</strong></p>
                <p>The CSA-National Cybersecurity R&amp;D Lab Scholarship (NCLS) aims to drive and support the development of professionals in Singapore’s cybersecurity sector and support the National Cybersecurity R&amp;D Lab (NCL) initiative. This scholarship (funded by the <a href="https://www.csa.gov.sg/">Cyber Security Agency of Singapore (CSA)</a>) will be awarded to qualified students who are enrolled in NUS’s Master’s programmes focusing on cybersecurity.</p>
                <p>&nbsp;</p>

                <p><strong>ELIGIBILITY</strong></p>
                <p>This scholarship is open to students who:</p>
                <ul>
                    <li>Are Singaporeans or Singapore Permanent Residents</li>
                    <li>Are admitted into NUS Masters programme with emphasis on cybersecurity (full-time or part-time)</li>
                </ul>
                <p>&nbsp;</p>

                <p><strong>BENEFITS OF NCLS PROGRAMME</strong></p>
                <ul>
                    <li>Tuition fees (after MOE Tuition Fee Grant) of NUS Master of Computing or related course programme</li>
                    <li>Monthly stipend of $2,000 (full-time students only), capped at 18 months</li>
                    <li>A project grant of $5,000 to the academic supervisors of the students’ cybersecurity projects or dissertations</li>
                </ul>
                <p>&nbsp;</p>
                
                <p><strong>CONDITIONS OF NCLS</strong></p>
                <p>Students who are awarded the scholarship must:</p>
                <ul>
                    <li>Maintain their Cumulative Average Point (CAP) at average 4.0 and have good academic standing </li>
                    <li>Post-graduation, be employed at NCL for a period of at least 1 year, or embark on their PhD studies in a Singapore University, or be employed in Singapore’s cybersecurity industry for at least 1 year</li>
                </ul>
                <p>&nbsp;</p>
                
                <p><strong>ELIGIBLE NUS MASTER’S PROGRAMMES</strong></p>
                <ul>
                    <li>Master of Computing (General Track) and working on either a dissertation, or a 2-semester project related to cybersecurity</li>
                    <li>Master of Computing (Computer Science Specialisation) and working on either a dissertation, or a 2-semester project related to cybersecurity</li>
                    <li>Master of Computing (Infocomm Security Specialisation)</li>
                    <li>Master programmes (by Research) from Faculty of Science with dissertation focused on cybersecurity</li>
                    <li>Master programmes (by Research) from Faculty of Engineering with dissertation focused on cybersecurity</li>
                </ul>
